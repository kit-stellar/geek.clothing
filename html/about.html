About Us

Welcome to Geek Clothing Co, a cultural hub and platform founded in 2020 dedicated to celebrating individuality and creativity. Geek is inspired by the word itself, which refers to a person who is knowledgeable about and is obsessively interested in a specific subject. Our logo embodies the essence of our brandâ€”a happy and cool nerdy kid. It symbolizes the joy of embracing one's unique interests and passions. At Geek, we embrace niche interests and underground art scenes, providing a voice for indie artists and passionate enthusiasts who often go unnoticed in mainstream media. Through our themed releases, we curate a diverse range of products that reflect various art and lifestyle influences, each meticulously designed to tell a story of their own for our community. Our goal is to inspire artists to break out of their comfort zones and embrace their uniqueness without fear of judgment. Join us as we strive to establish Geek as a recognized brand in Nueva Ecija and beyond while advocating for a more inclusive and diverse artistic landscape.
